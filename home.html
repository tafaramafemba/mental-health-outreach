Mental Health Awareness Global Summit 2022

A global yearly event that is centred on bringing mental health awareness at home, the workplace and social settings

2022.10.15(THU) ~ 16(FRI)

@ Madison Square Gardens, New York

Main Pogram

Presentations

Listen to mental health advodates present their views on mental health according to specialty.

Groups

Get into groups to have more intimate discussions and share ideas.

Counselling

Get a chance to talk to a counsellor in the event that you feel the need to throughout the Summit

Feedback

Give feedback on how the session went and where you may need help adjusting.

social

Network with other mental health professionals and widen the scope of your work.
